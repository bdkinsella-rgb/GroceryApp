Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share my grocery list with someone?

Tap the people icon in the top-left corner of the main screen. If you have items in your list, the app will create a share link you can send to anyone with an iCloud account. Once they accept, you'll both see the same list and can add or edit items.

My items aren't syncing between devices. What should I do?

Make sure both devices are signed into iCloud and have an internet connection. iCloud sync can sometimes take a minute or two. Try opening the app on both devices and waiting briefly. If the issue persists, go to Settings and check that iCloud is enabled for the app in your device's Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.

How do I remove someone from my shared list?

Tap the people icon to open the Collaboration screen, then tap "Invite People..." to open the sharing management view. From there you can remove participants or change their permissions.

How do I delete all my data?

Go to Settings (gear icon) > Danger Zone > Delete My Data. This permanently removes all your grocery items, photos, and iCloud data. If you are sharing a list, all participants will lose access.

Can I use the app without iCloud?

Yes. The app works fully offline and stores data locally on your device. However, sharing and syncing between devices require an iCloud account.

What does the "Need to Buy" filter show?

It shows items whose on-hand quantity has dropped to or below the restock threshold percentage of their target quantity. You can adjust this threshold in Settings.

How do I attach a photo to an item?

Tap on any item to open the edit screen, then scroll down to the Photo section. You can take a new photo with your camera or choose one from your photo library.
