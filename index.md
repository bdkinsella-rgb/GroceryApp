# Privacy Policy

**GroceryApp**  
Last updated: March 19, 2026

## Overview
GroceryApp is a grocery list and household inventory app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
GroceryApp does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third parties.

## Data Storage
All grocery list data (item names, quantities, categories, photos, and notes) is stored exclusively in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

## Sharing
If you choose to share your grocery list with another person, the data is shared directly between iCloud accounts using Apple's CloudKit sharing feature. The developer cannot see or access shared data.

## Analytics & Tracking
GroceryApp does **not** use any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

## Third-Party Services
GroceryApp does **not** integrate with any third-party services. The only external service used is Apple's iCloud/CloudKit, governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: KindersDevelopment@icloud.com
