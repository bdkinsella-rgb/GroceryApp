GroceryApp Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

GroceryApp ("the App") is developed by Kinders Development. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and how you can manage or delete it.

Data We Collect

The App collects the following data, which you provide directly:

  • Display Name — The name you enter in Settings. This is attached to grocery items you create so collaborators can see who added each item.
  • Photos — Images you optionally attach to grocery items using your device's camera or photo library.
  • Grocery Item Data — Item names, categories, quantities, units, notes, and stock status that you enter into the App.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. Your data is stored in Apple's iCloud infrastructure and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

We do not operate any servers. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Sharing and Collaboration

If you choose to share your grocery list using the App's collaboration feature, your grocery item data (including item names, photos, and your display name) will be visible to the people you explicitly invite. Sharing is initiated entirely by you through Apple's CloudKit sharing system. We have no access to or control over shared data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sent to any server other than Apple's iCloud.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing or search history
  • Purchase or financial information

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all of your data at any time from within the App:

  1. Open Settings (gear icon)
  2. Scroll to Danger Zone
  3. Tap Delete My Data

This permanently removes all grocery items, photos, and associated data from your device and from iCloud. If you were sharing a list, all participants will lose access.

You can also delete your iCloud data independently by going to your device's Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage > GroceryApp.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. The App does not require account creation and stores data only in the user's own iCloud account.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
